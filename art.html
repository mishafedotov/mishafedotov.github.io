The Moscow River Age / Triennale di Milano

My task was to create 4 abstract music compositions, each with a duration of 2 hours, to imagine how the Moscow river would sound in 2070, and what sounds there would be heard. In the future people will stop using the Moscow River for industrial purposes and will become more environmentally responsible. Therefore, in 2070 the water in Moscow river will be clean and suitable for recreation. To reproduce the atmosphere of the future, I collected sounds of industrial machines, demolition hammers, drills, motors, computers and transformed them with effects. About project: https://meganom.moscow/project/the-moscow-river-age/
