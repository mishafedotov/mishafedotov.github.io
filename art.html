SAGE OF THE SEA

Audio guide to the restaurant "Okhotka"

Scope of work: Music creation, sound design, voice mixing and processing

Okhotka is a seafood restaurant in Moscow. I was invited to participate in the creation of an audio guide for the flagship restaurant. My task was to create nine small musical compositions with actors' voices. The interior of the restaurant was supposed to be very minimalistic, with lots of art objects. With this in mind, I created a minimalistic melody on the piano, and added samples of nature sounds and sound effects.

About project: izi.travel
